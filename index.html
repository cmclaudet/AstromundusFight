About

Astromundus Fight is a 2D side scrolling fighting game built in the AWESOME Love2D framework. All you need is a keyboard and a friend to play. It features 4 playable characters each with unique characteristics. Check the links below to download now!

Mouseover or tap on any character.

The most versatile and reliable fighter, Cat can take on anyone, anytime, anywhere. Need range? She has it. Damage? Absolutely. Speed? This as well. Although few weaknesses may mean few strengths, she is the only fighter who is able to ignore damage entirely - She just needs a few drinks to properly drown it out...

Known for his agility and dexterity, Luis is the most popular fighter among fans who want to beat their enemy with pure speed! Be wary of his kick, Luis has spent many of his years mastering Taekwondo, and his battle skills certainly show it. Don’t be fooled by his laziness… To Luis, napping is the best way to “power up”, even in the middle of a fight!

This big guy will scare anyone rearing up for battle. Laurence is intimidating, brutal, and most of all, a massive tank. Hit after hit, you’ll still be dealing with this guy where others would have been long gone! Grab a mobile fighter who you can out maneuver Laurence, and maybe the fight will be yours. Just be careful you don’t let him get his hands on any cheese.

She’s determined and dead set on victory; Tadeja absolutely refuses to lose. Although she might not have the farthest reach of the group, her sheer strength is matched by none. Make sure you give it your all to dodge everything you can. Oh, and resist temptation - Tadeja is known to distract enemies with the sweet baked goods she makes!
